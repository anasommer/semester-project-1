Community Science Museum

Explore science together

For Kids

Come on down to our museum, there’s plenty to see and do. We also have the Young Stars club which meets once a week on a Saturday between 10:00 and 13:00 where you’ll get to explore and experiment with our team of experts. Each holiday we pick a new theme to explore. To find out more about the holiday club and how you can join, send us a message.
